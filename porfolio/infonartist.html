Tasha Newton was born in Colorado in 1990. She thrived in art classes and very much loved playing with clay in Elementary. Entering into high school, she took jewelry classes and mixed drawing classes that worked in different mediums for all four years of school. Graduating High School with a 4.0 GPA in 2008. She decided to go to Metropolitan State College work towards an Art Degree, although she felt that wasn't quite a fit for her and changed her degree. She decided on a Criminal Justice Major, while holding onto her art side she applied for a Digital Media Minor. She chose Digital Media, because of her fascination with Photoshop, although she loves working in all kinds of art. She has worked with pastel, watercolor, clay, ink, charcoal, origami, pencil, spray paint, photography, and different digital mediums. She is estimated to graduate with 3.8 cumative GPA in December of 2015, with a Bachelor's Degree in Criminal Justice.
