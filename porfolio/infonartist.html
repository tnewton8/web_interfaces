Tasha Newton was born in Colorado in 1990. She thrived in art classes and very much loved playing with clay in Elementry. Entering into high school, she took jewelry classes and mixed drawing classes that worked in different mediums for all four years of school. Graduating high school in 2008, she went to Metropolitan State College thinking of going into a Art Degree, felt that wasn't quite a fit for her. She then became an Criminal Justice Major, while holding onto her art side she applied for a Digital Media Minor. She chose Digital Media, because of her facination with Photoshope, although she loves working in any medium of art. She has worked with pastel, watercolor, clay, ink, charcoal, oragomi, pencil, spray paint, photography, and different digital mediums.
